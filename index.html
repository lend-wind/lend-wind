Frequently asked questions

To participate in Lendwind, you need a Web3 wallet, with Metamask currently being the only supported option.

Additionally, you need to purchase USDT and BNB. USDT is used for your deposit, while BNB is required to pay for transaction fees on the Binance Smart Chain when interacting with our Smart Contract.

The easiest way to add the Binance Smart Chain network to Metamask is by visiting BscScan dot com and clicking on the "Add BSC Network" button located at the bottom left corner of the site. This will automatically configure your Metamask to interact with the Binance Smart Chain.

Metamask is one of the most trusted Web3 wallets and is essential for interacting with our Smart Contract. It allows you to securely sign transactions and transfer your coins to the Smart Contract, ensuring a seamless and safe experience.

You can purchase BNB and USDT from any centralized exchange or wallet that supports the Binance Smart Chain network and BEP20 tokens. Some popular options include Binance, Bybit, OKK, KuCoin, and Kraken.

If you already have USDT and BNB, follow these steps:

1. Download and install the Metamask browser extension.

2. Add the Binance Smart Chain network to Metamask.

3. Transfer your USDT and BNB from your exchange to your Metamask wallet address.

4. Complete the Approval and Deposit steps on this site.

The loan process is fully automated by our Smart Contract. Once you deposit USDT into the pool, your Metamask wallet address is added to a queue. Your balance is tracked on a ledger, and when the pool is sufficiently funded, a transfer to your account will be triggered automatically.

The timeframe for receiving your loan can vary. It may take a few hours, a day, or possibly up to a week. The loan will be automatically transferred to your account once the pool reaches the necessary funding level. We encourage you to invite friends to join Lendwind to help accelerate loan disbursements and grow our supportive community.
